GOATs of Basketball

Michael Jordan

Michael Jeffrey Jordan (born February 17, 1963 in Brooklyn, New York) is an American basketball player and former NBA player. The best basketball player in history (according to ESPN experts). He played as an attacking defender. Jordan was instrumental in popularizing basketball and the NBA around the world in the 1980s and 1990s. Two-time Olympic champion.

LeBron James

LeBron Raymone James (English: LeBron Raymone James; b. December 30, 1984 Akron, Summit, Ohio, USA) is an American basketball player who plays as a light and heavy forward. He plays for the NBA team Los Angeles Lakers. He is recognized by experts as one of the best basketball players in history, and a number of experts put James in the first place. One of the highest paid athletes in the world for most of his career
